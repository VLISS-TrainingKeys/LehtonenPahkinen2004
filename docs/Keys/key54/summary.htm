Training key 54: The role of auxiliary information in PPS sampling

SUMMARY OF THE MONTE CARLO SIMULATION STUDY ON PPSWOR ESTIMATOR

 

The summary of descriptive statistics of the Monte Carlo simulation study is viewed in the following table. The results are based on 1000 simulated samples of 8 municipalities from the Province´91 population of 32 municipalities. Properties of SRSWOR_HT estimator and PPSWOR estimator with auxiliary variables HOU85, X and Z are examined. The role of auxiliary information becomes quite clear. If a suitable auxiliary variable (such as HOU85) is used, efficient estimation in a PPS sample is achieved. Weak auxiliary information used in PPS sampling provides inefficient estimation and is not much better than SRSWOR estimation strategy. In some cases (such as with Z used as auxiliary variable in this case study) PPS estimation produces less efficient estimates than SRSWOR. This is due to the negative correlation of Z and UE91.
